// 01 — About

Where neural data meets
mathematical rigour

The CompNeuro Workshop brings together graduate students, postdocs, and faculty for an immersive week of lectures, hands-on labs, and collaborative research discussions.

Participants gain practical experience with modern techniques for analysing neural population activity, fitting mechanistic models, and applying deep learning to neuroscience problems.

All tutorial notebooks and datasets are open-source and available on GitHub after the workshop.

  • Dimensionality reduction of neural population data
  • Latent variable models & variational inference
  • Spiking neural networks & STDP
  • Reinforcement learning & decision making
  • Recurrent network dynamics
  • Bayesian decoding of sensory signals
  • Point process models for spike trains
  • Large-scale neural data with Neuropixels
